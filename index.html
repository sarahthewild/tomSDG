TOM's SDG presence

The below graph displays the SDG presence of the TOM faculty in terms of research and teaching. The 'presence' is portrayed as the sum of the faculties' interest and contribution for each SDG. You can highlight an SDG by clicking on a flower's leaf or on the SDG symbol above.


TOM's SDG network

The below graph displays the research connections of the TOM faculty. You can hide or display the interconnections between the different SDGs, i.e. the secondary links, by clicking on the toggle below.
To highlight the links of respective faculty or SDGs, simply click on the node. To undo the highlight, double click on the node.
